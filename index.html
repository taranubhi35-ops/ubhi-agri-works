Our Establishment

About UBHI AGRI WORKS

Serving the Farming Community Since 1965

UBHI AGRI WORKS is a trusted name in agricultural equipment and services located in Bassian Bet, Ludhiana. For over five decades, we have been providing farmers with reliable machinery, equipment rentals, and expert repair services.

With generations of experience in the agricultural sector, we understand the unique challenges faced by farmers and provide solutions that enhance productivity and efficiency.

Proprietor: SANTOKH SINGH

Continuing the family legacy, Santokh Singh has built upon the foundation laid in 1965, making UBHI AGRI WORKS a trusted partner for farmers across Punjab.
